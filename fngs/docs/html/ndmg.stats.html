ndmg.stats.func_qa_utils.mse(imageA, imageB)¶

the ‘Mean Squared Error’ between the two images is the sum of the squared difference between the two images; NOTE: the two images must have the same dimension from http://www.pyimagesearch.com/2014/09/15/python-compare-two-images/ NOTE: we’ve normalized the signals by the mean intensity at each point to make comparisons btwn fMRI and MPRAGE more viable. Otherwise, fMRI signal vastly overpowers MPRAGE signal and our MSE would have very low accuracy (fMRI signal differences >>> actual differences we are looking for).

Positional Arguments:
imageA:
  • the first image.
imageB:
  • the second image.
